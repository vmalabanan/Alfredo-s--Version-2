Once upon a time...

There was a boy named Alfredo who craved a burrito.

Not just any burrito, but the kind stuffed with carne asada and fries like they had in his hometown, San Diego.

He searched far and wide but couldn't find one, so he decided to make his own.

He experimented and perfected it until he got it right: a handmade tortilla stuffed with carne asada, sour cream, cheese, and fresh cut fries, just like home.

When he grew up, he wanted to share the food that had brought him so much joy.

And so, Alfredo's was born...
